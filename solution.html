AegisCare:Smart Post-Op Wound Monitoring

Our Solution

AegisCare is a real-time wound monitoring system designed for patients recovering at home after surgery and for diabetic foot ulcer care. Instead of relying on visual checks and guesswork, AegisCare continuously tracks wound conditions and flags early signs of risk.
